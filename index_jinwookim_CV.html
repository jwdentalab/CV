About

Oral and maxillofacial surgeon and researcher whose work bridges clinical surgery, bone metabolism, and artificial intelligence. Board-certified specialist (Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery; Integrated Dentistry) and Fellow of the International Board for the Certification of Specialists in OMS (FIBCSOMS). Research centers on medication-related osteonecrosis of the jaw (MRONJ), multi-omics diagnostics, and deep-learning models for dental and craniofacial imaging. 임상 수술·골대사·인공지능을 잇는 구강악안면외과 전문의이자 연구자. 구강악안면외과 및 통합치의학과 전문의이며 국제구강악안면외과 전문의(FIBCSOMS). 약물관련 턱뼈괴사(MRONJ), 멀티오믹스 진단, 치과·두개안면 영상 딥러닝 모델을 중심으로 연구한다.

Focus areas: MRONJ & bone metabolism · dental & craniofacial AI · multi-omics precision diagnostics · 3D-printed patient-specific devices · cleft & airway. 중점 분야: MRONJ·골대사 · 치과/두개안면 AI · 멀티오믹스 정밀진단 · 3D 프린팅 맞춤형 의료기기 · 구순구개열·기도.
